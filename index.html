NMIXX (엔믹스) is a six-member girl group under JYP Entertainment. Originally as seven, they debuted on February 22, 2022 with the single album "Ad Mare".

Their name combines the letter "N", which stands for "now", "new", "next", and the unknown "n", and the word "mix", which symbolizes combination and diversity, altogether meaning "the best combination for a new era".
